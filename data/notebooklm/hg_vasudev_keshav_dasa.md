### austerity, devotional service, Krishna consciousness

**Summary:** HG Vasudev Keshav Dasa explains that true austerity is centered around devotional service to Krishna, not material gain, and that worshiping Krishna is the highest form of Tapasya.

Vrishabhadeva said to his sons that Tapo Divyam Putraka Yena Satvam Shuddhe, heart will become cleansed by practicing austerities. And we have some very simple austerities to practice: rising early in the morning, chanting 16 rounds, taking morning bath. All our austerities are actually centered around devotional service. The goal of austerity is service to Krishna, not to attain some power or some material opulence. Tapasa, you are doing Tapasya. If you are doing worship of Krishna, that is the highest Tapasya. There is no need of any other higher Tapasya. And if you are not engaged in devotional service, then all of you will be in trouble. Any other so-called Tapasya we may be doing, like sometimes some people say, "I am doing this Vrata, I am doing that Vrata, I am doing Somvar Vrata or I am..." All that is good, but it is only at a material plane. We follow Ekadashi to please Krishna. That's why if you see, all the Tapasyas and Krishna consciousness in our life is all aimed towards furthering our devotional service.
